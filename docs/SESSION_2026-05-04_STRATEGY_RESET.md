# Session 2026-05-04 Strategy Reset

## Main Decision

The project should explicitly return to a business-centered AI/automation positioning.

`AI bez slozhnosti` stays the public-facing brand, but the real territory is:

- AI for small business
- bots
- CRM
- automation
- content systems

This is not a generic AI education channel and not a broad prompt/news channel.

## Core Positioning

Nikolay is positioned as:

- a practical translator between AI and business
- a specialist who helps diagnose where AI is useful
- a person who can then build a working system:
  - bot
  - CRM link
  - AI assistant
  - automation flow
  - content automation

## Core Audience

Primary audience:

- entrepreneurs
- small business owners
- experts
- marketers
- consultants
- small teams
- non-technical Russia/CIS users who tried AI superficially and did not yet see a real business result

Typical state:

- heard about AI
- tried ChatGPT or analogs at surface level
- asked random questions
- got weak output
- concluded AI is vague, overhyped or not for their business
- still suspect there is practical value somewhere

## Main Lead Magnet

The main lead magnet must be a free AI business audit/helper bot.

The bot asks 7 short business questions and returns:

- diagnosis
- 3 automation priorities
- quick win for this week
- suitable tools
- approximate ROI

Target funnel:

1. post
2. interest / recognition
3. free AI audit
4. personal result
5. dialogue
6. consultation / automation map / MVP / implementation

## Content Strategy

The channel should not mainly explain "what AI is".

It should show:

- where business loses time manually
- where leads are lost
- where routine creates chaos
- where follow-up is weak
- where no system exists
- how AI, bots, CRM and automation can help

## Updated Content Mix

The previous broader AI-public-education weighting was too loose for the current strategy.

Updated default mix:

- `practical`: 60
- `educational`: 20
- `news`: 10
- `opinion`: 5
- `critical`: 5

Interpretation:

- `practical` = business situations, automation ideas, cases, build-in-public, applied scenarios
- `educational` = simple AI explanations and useful AI tricks for work/business routine
- `news` = AI updates with practical business takeaway
- `opinion` = human, thoughtful, backstage or author-angle interpretation
- `critical` = AI without illusions, limits, risks, where not to automate

## Product Principle

This strategy should not live only in manual product settings.

It should be partially hardcoded as the base operating principle of the content factory.

Reason:

- the strategy is foundational, not optional
- the factory should default toward this positioning even if brief/context is imperfect
- strong anti-rules reduce drift into low-value content

## Hardcoded Prompt Rules Added

The base prompt logic was updated in code for both:

- plan generation
- post generation

Added base strategy behavior:

- business-centered AI/automation framing
- real business-situation recognition
- movement toward free AI audit
- preference for diagnosis / implementation help over self-assembly

Added anti-rules:

- no DIY default framing
- no no-code evangelism as the default answer
- no fake personal stories
- no abstract AI hype
- no technical overload for non-technical readers
- no generic AI news without practical value
- no random-tool enthusiasm without process diagnosis
